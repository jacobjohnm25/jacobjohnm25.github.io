JobsMePlatform - Jobs Made easy

Project Description & Results.

The Client

JobsMe platform(Jobs Made Easy) is way for Agents to get Professional Services as quickly as possible. A technology to create a solution that helps both sides (Agents & Vendors) to scale their business and better support Customers.

The Proposal

I was tasked to handle the entire design process from web to mobile(ios and Android).I was provided with the company logo.The client wanted a simple but intutive design.

The Results

The Mobile version of this application was released after a long gap. The App is inteded for the mobile platform basically although I did design and commit a web version also.
